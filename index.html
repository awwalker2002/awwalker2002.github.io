Playoffs Success

Introduction

The NBA finals are one of the most watched sporting events in the world, with viewership peaking at 16.9 million people during the 2022 NBA finals. As an NBA fan and casual sports bettor, I was interested in seeing what the biggest indicators of success in the NBA playoffs are and see if I could use those indicators to narrow down the list of potential NBA champions for the 2022-2023 season. I collected the advanced statistics for each NBA season from the 2011-2012 season to the 2021-2022 season from Basketball Reference and conducted an analysis on the data to hopefully gain some insight into what makes an NBA champion.



Offensive and Defensive
Efficiency of NBA Champions

As a starting point, I was interested in seeing the offensive and defensive efficiency ratings of previous championship winners, and whether or not there have been any trends that can be observed over the past 11 seasons. To investigate this, I created a line plot that visualizes the offensive and defensive efficiencies of NBA champions, and as shown in the plot, the offensive efficiency of NBA champions has been on an upward trend while the defensive efficiency of NBA champions has been on a downward trend. This is something that we would expect to see, as teams have become more offensive minded over the last few seasons. Keeping the information shown in the plot in mind, we can expect this years NBA champion to have a defensive efficiency rating between approximately 106 and 112 and an offensive efficiency rating that is greater than 112.
